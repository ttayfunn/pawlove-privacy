PawLove Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

```

PawLove is a pet care and pet information management app operated by Tayfun Baş. This Privacy Policy explains what information PawLove collects, how it is used, and how users can contact us regarding their data.

Information We Collect

PawLove may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information, such as email address and user ID
  • Pet profile information entered by the user
  • Photos, documents, and other content uploaded by the user
  • Location information when the user creates or uses location-based features, such as lost pet reports
  • Purchase or subscription status related to PawLove Plus
  • Technical information required for app functionality and security

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Store and display pet profiles
  • Save pet-related photos, documents, microchip, passport, or similar records
  • Help users create and manage lost pet reports
  • Provide PawLove Plus subscription features
  • Improve app reliability, security, and functionality
  • Respond to support or privacy-related requests

User-Generated Content

Users may upload pet photos, documents, and other pet-related information. Users are responsible for the content they upload and should only upload content they have the right to use.

Location Data

PawLove may request access to location data for features such as lost pet reports or nearby pet-related functionality. Location data is used only for app functionality and is not used for advertising or tracking.

In-App Purchases

PawLove offers optional in-app purchases and subscriptions through Apple’s App Store. Payment processing is handled by Apple. PawLove may use purchase or subscription status to enable premium features.

Third-Party Services

PawLove may use third-party services such as Firebase and Apple services for authentication, data storage, app functionality, and in-app purchases. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Tracking and Advertising

PawLove does not use user data for third-party advertising tracking. PawLove does not sell personal data.

Data Retention

We keep user data as long as necessary to provide the app’s features, maintain account functionality, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Users may request deletion of their data.

Data Deletion Requests

Users may request account or data deletion by contacting us at: review@pawlove.app

Children’s Privacy

PawLove is not specifically directed to children. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete that information.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect user data. However, no digital service can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: review@pawlove.app
```
